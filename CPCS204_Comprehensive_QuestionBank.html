About This Booklet

This document is a comprehensive revision booklet for CPCS-204 (Data Structures I). It is organised as a question bank across twelve parts, ranging from foundational concepts to advanced tracing problems, and ends with an Answer Key & Hint Sheet. The intent is to expose you to every recurring style of question that may appear on midterms and finals.

Each question is tagged with a difficulty label: EASY MEDIUM HARD. Diagrams accompany many questions to illustrate the underlying data structure or algorithm. The booklet is optimised for both screen reading and A4 printing.

Note (screen view only): Tree, heap, list, and graph diagrams are rendered using Mermaid. If a diagram appears blank, ensure the page has internet access on first load, then refresh.
